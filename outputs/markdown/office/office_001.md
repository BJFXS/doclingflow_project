**Title of Invention: Sample with ordered lists and bullets**

- This sample shows how use ordered/numbered lists and bullet lists:
- Normal Numbering:

1. Level 1 - Numbering 1
2. Level 1 - Numbering 2
- 2.1. Level 2 - Numbering 1 of Level 1 - Numbering .... 2
- 2.2. Level 2 - Numbering 2 of Level 1 - Numbering .... 2
- 2.2.1. Level 3 – Numbering 1 of Level 2 – Numbering .... 2
- 2.2.2. Level 3 – Numbering 2 of Level 2 – Numbering .... 2

- ] Numbering lists with combination of different levels.

1. Level 1 - Numbering 1
2. Level 1 - Numbering 2
- 2.1. Level 2 - Numbering 1 of Level 1 - Numbering .... 2
